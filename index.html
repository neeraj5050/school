About Us

Rajpur Public School is a dedicated educational institution offering quality education from Nursery to Class 8. Our school provides a nurturing environment where students can grow both academically and personally. We are proud to have a spacious playground that encourages physical activity, teamwork, and overall development. The school is managed by a team of highly qualified and committed faculty members who strive to bring out the best in every child. We also offer safe and reliable bus facilities for students commuting from nearby areas. The campus is peaceful and secure, creating an ideal atmosphere for learning. Under the leadership of Principal Sir Rakesh Karil and Principal Mam Shakila Shayad, the school continues to uphold high standards of education and discipline
